Transform the way you share information with QRGenerator, the professional QR code creation tool designed for iOS. Whether you're sharing WiFi credentials with guests, creating event invitations, or generating website links, QRGenerator makes it effortless.

KEY FEATURES

• URL QR CODES
Generate scannable links to websites, online menus, social media profiles, or any web destination. Perfect for business cards, marketing materials, and contact-free information sharing.

• WIFI QR CODES
Share your WiFi network instantly without typing passwords. Guests simply scan the code to connect automatically. Supports WPA, WEP, and open networks.

• CALENDAR EVENT QR CODES
Create shareable event invitations with full details including title, location, date, time, and description. Recipients can add events to their calendar with a single scan.

• SMS QR CODES
Pre-populate text messages with custom recipients and message content. Great for text-to-vote campaigns, customer support shortcuts, and automated messaging.

• EMAIL QR CODES
Generate mailto codes with pre-filled recipient, subject, and body text. Streamline customer inquiries and contact forms.
